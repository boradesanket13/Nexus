Fun facts about Venus

─ Venus is the hottest planet in our solar system. It’s so hot the metal lead would melt.

─ It also has no moons and spins backwards compared to the other planets. This means that the sun rises in the west and sets in the east on Venus.

─ If you weigh 45 kg (100 pounds) on Earth, you would weigh 41 kg (91 pounds) on Venus.
